Banana Bread

wet ingredients

  • 2-3 over ripe bananas
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup melted butter

dry ingredients

  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup of flour

instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Add bananas to bowl and mash until pasty
  3. Mix wet ingredients with mashed bananas
  4. In seperate bowl, mix dry ingredients well
  5. Add mixed dry ingredients to wet ingredients
  6. grease bread pan to prevent sticking
  7. Add mixture to bread pan
  8. Bake for 50min-1 hour
  9. Test if it is done by inserting toothpick. If toothpick is clean, let cool and enjoy!
