Chocolate Pudding

ingredients

  • 1 tab of butter
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup corn starch
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 2 1/2 cup of milk
    • 2 squares unsweetened chocolate

instructions

  1. mix sugar, cornstarch and salt in saucepan
  2. gradually stir in milk until smooth
  3. cook over medium heat until mixture comes to a boil
  4. let boil 1 minute, then remove from heat
  5. still half of the hot mixture into egg yolks, pour back into sauce pan
  6. add choclate
  7. bring back to a boil for 1 minute
  8. remove from heat
  9. stir in butter and vanilla extract
  10. let cool and enjoy!
